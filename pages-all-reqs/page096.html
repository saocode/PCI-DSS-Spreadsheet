Key-management processes and procedures
(Requirements 3.6 and 3.7) do not apply to system
components used to generate individual keyed hashes
of a PAN for comparison to another system if:
• The system components only have access to one
hash value at a time (hash values are not stored on
the system)
AND
• There is no other account data stored on the same
system as the hashes.
This requirement is considered a best practice until 31
March 2025, after which it will be required and must be
fully considered during a PCI DSS assessment. This
requirement will replace the bullet in Requirement 3.5.1
for one-way hashes once its effective date is reached.
